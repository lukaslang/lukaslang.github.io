About me

Hi, I'm Lukas!

I empower people with insights from data. I absolutely enjoy working in interdisciplinary teams and with people from a wide range of backgrounds.

I am Head of Data Science and AI at voestalpine High Performance Metals DIGITAL SOLUTIONS in Vienna, Austria, where I lead a team that builds data science and machine learning products, and supports our local data teams at the production sites in use case implementations.

My second role at voestalpine is (Data Science) Platform Lead, where I co-lead a global Data Science programme in the High Performance Metals Division.

Before transitioning to industry, I pursued a career in academic research. My research was in mathematical image analysis and inverse problems, and has applications in medical imaging, molecular and cell biology, and computer vision.

Feel free to get in touch with me by mail, follow me on Twitter, or check out my code on GitHub to learn more.
