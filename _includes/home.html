Biography

I am interested in maths, data, science, ...
2019-present
I am the Data Science & AI Lead at voestalpine High Performance Metals in Vienna, Austria.
During Winter term 2019, I was also an external lecturer at the Faculty of Informatics at TU Wien.
2017-2019
I held a position as a research associate within the Department of Applied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics (DAMTP) at the University of Cambridge, UK. I was a member of the Cambridge Image Analysis group and was supervised by Prof Dr Carola-Bibiane Schönlieb.
2015-2016
I was a research scientist at the Johann Radon Institute for Computational and Applied Mathematics (RICAM) and part of the Inverse Problems and Mathematical Imaging group.
2012-2015
I earned a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Vienna, where I was supervised by Univ-Prof Dr Otmar Scherzer. During my PhD studies I was a member of the Computational Science Center (CSC) and of the Vienna Graduate School in Computational Science. My thesis investigates Optical flow on evolving manifolds with an application to the analysis of fluorescence microscopy data.
2005-2012
I obtained a MSc from TU Wien in Computational Intelligence. My Master's thesis is on Image Denoising with Variational Methods via Graph Cuts and was supervised mainly by Univ-Prof Dr Otmar Scherzer. BSC at TU Wien in Software & Information Engineering. My Bachelor's thesis is on Metaheuristics for the multicast tree scheduling problem and was supervised by Priv-Doz Dr Nysret Musliu and Priv-Doz Dr Sandford Bessler.
